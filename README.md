## Contributing

We welcome contributions aligned with our **quality and regulatory commitments**.

1. **Open an Issue** describing problem/feature and regulatory impact (if any).  
2. **Branching:** `feature/<short-desc>`, `fix/<short-desc>`.  
3. **Commits:** Use conventional commits (e.g., `feat:`, `fix:`, `docs:`) with clear rationale.  
4. **Pull Requests:** Include design intent, risks, verification/validation notes, and **traceability** to requirements.  
5. **Reviews:** At least **2 reviewers**, including one **QA or Process Owner** for regulated repos.

> For questions, email **bvanorden@aust-mfg.com** (Org Maintainer).
